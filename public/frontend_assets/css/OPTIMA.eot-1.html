about Trinayan Homes Pvt. Ltd.

Boasting a 30-years legacy in real estate development and management, Trinayan Homes Pvt. Ltd. is a frontrunner in the industry. It's unmatched delivery scale and customer centric service excellence have cemented Trinayan Homes Pvt. Ltd. as a trusted name in India's dynamic real estate sector.

Trinayan Homes Pvt. Ltd. Land and Infrastructure Ltd., a leading Indian conglomerate, has been engaged in the development of real estate for the last three decades in Gurugram.
